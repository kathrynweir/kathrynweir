Rethinking Nature

MADRE Museum
16.12.2021 - 5.4.2022
An exhibition, book and series of programmes focussing on contemporary artistic research-based practices and critical thinking that are contributing to cultural and political processes centring on political ecology in different contexts in order to rethink and transform conceptions of nature bequeathed by Europe’s Enlightenment paradigms in parallel to its imperial expansion.
